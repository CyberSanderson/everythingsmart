🎯 How to Choose the Best 3D Printer

Consider these factors before you buy:

  • Budget: Prices range from <$200 to $5,000+
  • Print Size: Small hobby builds vs large professional prototypes
  • Features: Auto-leveling, enclosure, dual extrusion, Wi-Fi monitoring
  • Ease of Use: Pre-assembled vs DIY kits
  • Community Support: Vital for troubleshooting
