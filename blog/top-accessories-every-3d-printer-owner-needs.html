Top Accessories Every 3D Printer Owner Needs

Want to get the most out of your 3D printer? The right accessories can take your printing from good to exceptional. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned maker, these essential tools will improve print quality, safety, and workflow.

1. Quality Build Surface or Bed Adhesive

A reliable build surface or adhesive ensures your prints stick properly and come off cleanly.

  • PEI sheets or magnetic flex plates for easy part removal
  • Glue stick or hairspray as budget-friendly options

✅ Shop PEI Build Surfaces on Amazon

2. Precision Calipers

Accurate measurements are crucial for calibrating and verifying your prints.

  • Check filament diameter and part dimensions
  • Digital calipers make precise readings simple

✅ Shop Digital Calipers on Amazon

3. Nozzle Cleaning Kit

Keep your extruder running smoothly and prevent clogs with the right tools.

  • Needles and brushes for various nozzle sizes
  • Essential for routine maintenance

✅ Find Nozzle Cleaning Kits on Amazon

4. Spare Nozzles

Having extra nozzles on hand saves time and lets you experiment with different materials and print resolutions.

  • Consider hardened nozzles for abrasive filaments
  • Different diameters for speed vs. detail

✅ Browse Spare Nozzles on Amazon

5. Filament Storage Solutions

Keep your filament dry and protected to avoid moisture-related print failures.

  • Airtight containers with desiccant packs
  • Filament dryers to refresh spools

✅ Shop Filament Dryers on Amazon

6. Bed Leveling Tools

A level bed is the foundation of a successful print.

  • Feeler gauges for manual leveling
  • BLTouch sensors for automatic bed leveling

✅ Explore BLTouch Sensors on Amazon

7. Cleaning and Finishing Tools

Achieve professional results with basic post-processing tools.

  • Deburring tools and sanding sticks
  • Flush cutters for removing supports

✅ Find Finishing Tools on Amazon

8. OctoPrint or Remote Monitoring

Monitor and control your prints from anywhere with a smart setup.

  • Raspberry Pi for running OctoPrint
  • Wi-Fi-enabled printers for easier management

✅ Get Raspberry Pi for OctoPrint

Final Thoughts

These accessories aren't just optional—they can save you time, improve print quality, and make your 3D printing experience more enjoyable. Whether you’re setting up your first printer or upgrading your workshop, investing in the right tools will pay off every time you press “print.”
