Top Accessories Every 3D Printer Owner Needs

Want to get the most out of your 3D printer? The right accessories can turn a good printer into a great one. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned maker, these essential tools and upgrades will help improve your print quality, safety, and workflow.

1. Quality Build Surface or Bed Adhesive

Stop fighting bed adhesion issues. A good build surface or adhesive helps prints stick reliably.

  • PEI sheets or magnetic flex plates for easy removal
  • Glue stick or hairspray for budget-friendly adhesion

✅ Recommended: PEI Build Surface on Amazon

2. Precision Calipers

Measure your filament diameter, check part dimensions, and ensure your printer is dialed in.

  • Essential for calibrating flow rates and tolerance
  • Digital calipers offer easy, accurate readings

✅ Recommended: Digital Calipers on Amazon

3. Nozzle Cleaning Kit

Prevent clogs and keep extrusion smooth with cleaning tools or needles designed for your nozzle size.

  • Handy for routine maintenance
  • Works for various nozzle diameters

✅ Recommended: Nozzle Cleaning Kit on Amazon

4. Spare Nozzles

Always have backups on hand in case of wear, clogs, or switching materials.

  • Consider hardened nozzles for abrasive filaments
  • Experiment with different diameters for speed vs. detail

✅ Recommended: Spare Nozzles on Amazon

5. Filament Storage Solutions

Moisture ruins filament quality. Store it properly to avoid bad prints.

  • Dry boxes or airtight containers with desiccant
  • Filament dryers to restore compromised spools

✅ Recommended: Filament Dryer on Amazon

6. Bed Leveling Tools

Manual or auto—keeping your bed level is crucial for first-layer success.

  • Feeler gauges for manual leveling
  • BLTouch or other automatic sensors for upgrades

✅ Recommended: BLTouch Auto Bed Leveling Sensor

7. Cleaning and Finishing Tools

Improve your prints post-processing with basic finishing gear:

  • Deburring tools
  • Sanding sticks
  • Flush cutters for supports

✅ Recommended: 3D Print Finishing Tools on Amazon

8. OctoPrint or Remote Monitoring

Keep tabs on your printer from anywhere. OctoPrint setups let you watch, control, and stop prints remotely.

  • Raspberry Pi setups for DIY OctoPrint
  • Commercial Wi-Fi-enabled printers offer built-in options

✅ Recommended: Raspberry Pi for OctoPrint

Final Thoughts

These accessories aren’t just nice-to-have—they can save time, improve print quality, and make 3D printing more enjoyable. Whether you're just starting or looking to upgrade your setup, investing in the right tools will pay off every time you hit “print.”
